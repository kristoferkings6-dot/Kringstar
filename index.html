Only leads that answer all three with concrete, realistic numbers get booked as Qualified Appointments. No commitment = no booking.

PropertyPro gives you leads—phone numbers of people who may or may not be serious. We give you appointments—pre-screened, budget-confirmed, timeline-committed buyers who show up ready to make a decision. We can't close deals for you—but we guarantee you'll be sitting across from people who genuinely can buy.
